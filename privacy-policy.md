# Privacy Policy for d.ai.ly

**Last Updated:** November 15, 2025

## Introduction
d.ai.ly ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our mobile application.

## Data Collection and Storage
**We do NOT collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data on external servers.**

All data created and managed within d.ai.ly is stored **locally on your device only**, including:
- Tasks and to-do lists
- Income and expense records
- Budget information
- Meeting schedules
- Notes and reminders
- Pomodoro timer sessions

## Data We Do NOT Collect
- Personal identification information
- Location data
- Device information
- Usage analytics
- Crash reports
- Any other personal data

## App Permissions
d.ai.ly may request the following permissions:

### Camera Permission (Optional)
- **Purpose:** For potential future AI-powered features
- **Usage:** Only when you explicitly choose to use camera features
- **Data:** No photos or camera data are stored or transmitted

### Calendar Permission (Optional)
- **Purpose:** To integrate meeting scheduling with your device calendar
- **Usage:** Only for reading/writing calendar events you create in the app
- **Data:** Calendar data stays on your device

### Storage Permission
- **Purpose:** To import/export data in CSV format
- **Usage:** Only when you choose to import or export your financial data
- **Data:** Files remain under your control on your device

## Third-Party Services
We do **NOT** use:
- Analytics services
- Advertising networks
- Crash reporting tools
- Cloud storage services
- Social media integrations

## Data Security
Since all data is stored locally on your device:
- Your data is protected by your device's security measures
- We recommend enabling device encryption and screen lock
- No data transmission means no risk of interception
- You control all backups through your device's backup system

## Data Sharing
We do **NOT** share your data with:
- Third-party companies
- Advertisers
- Analytics providers
- Any other entities

Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it.

## Children's Privacy
d.ai.ly is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app contains no content intended for children.

## Your Rights
Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:
- **Access:** All your data is accessible within the app
- **Deletion:** Uninstalling the app removes all data
- **Export:** Use the CSV export feature to back up your data
- **Portability:** Export your data to use in other applications

## Data Retention
Data is retained on your device until you:
- Manually delete it within the app
- Clear the app's data in device settings
- Uninstall the application

## Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:
- Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
- Updating the "Last Updated" date
- Notifying through app updates when significant changes occur

Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## International Users
d.ai.ly can be used worldwide. Since all data is stored locally, there are no international data transfers.
